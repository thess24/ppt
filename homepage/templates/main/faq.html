Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to start selling?

If you already have a Stripe account (our payment processor) it only takes about 5 minutes! Setting up a Stripe account takes another 5 minutes and only has to be done once.

How does buying work?

You do not need an account to buy. Simply click "Buy" on the purchase page, enter your billing information, and download. You will recieve an email with a link to your purchase that can be used 5 times. If you do create an account, we keep track of your purchases for you so you do not need to save the email. The entire site is secured with business grade SSL encryption and because of our excellent payment processor, Stripe, we never even see your payment data.

When is the site updated?

Every day! When someone submits new content it will be reviewed and put online the next day.

How do I get paid?

Stripe, the payment processor, collects payments from customers and distributes the funds on a rolling weekly basis. This means you get paid one week after the customer buys your product. You make 70% of each sale after card processing fees!

How much should I sell my presentation/items for?

It is recommended to sell for at least $10-$15 USD.

Why should I get an account?

Getting an account lets you save your files for easier access! You do not need an account to buy.
